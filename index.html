Privacy Policy

Last updated: 24 June 2026

SrintellX ("we", "our", "us") operates an AI-powered reception system that helps clinics manage patient calls and WhatsApp inquiries. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you interact with us through WhatsApp, our website, or our services.

1. Information We Collect

When you interact with SrintellX through WhatsApp or our website, we may collect:

  • Your name and phone number (as provided via WhatsApp)
  • Clinic name, specialty, and city
  • Messages you send to our WhatsApp business number
  • Information you share about your clinic's communication needs
  • Demo booking details (date, time, email)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected to:

  • Respond to your inquiries about SrintellX services
  • Schedule and conduct product demonstrations
  • Understand clinic communication challenges to recommend relevant solutions
  • Follow up on your interest in our services
  • Improve our products and customer experience

3. AI-Powered Communication

Our WhatsApp assistant uses AI to respond to your messages. Conversations are processed to provide relevant information about our services. AI responses are based on approved company information and are not used to make automated decisions that affect your rights.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:

  • Service providers who help us operate our platform (hosting, AI processing, messaging infrastructure)
  • Meta Platforms, Inc. as required to operate WhatsApp Business services
  • Google, for calendar integration when scheduling demos

All service providers are bound by data protection obligations.

5. Data Storage and Security

Your information is stored on secure, encrypted servers. We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your data against unauthorised access, alteration, or destruction.

6. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide our services and fulfil the purposes described in this policy. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information
  • Withdraw consent for further communication

8. WhatsApp and Third-Party Platforms

When you message us on WhatsApp, Meta's own privacy policy also applies to how your messages are transmitted. We encourage you to review Meta's privacy policy separately.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
